executive summary

54 Republic is a content driven Digital Media Communication agency. We are (ownership) e.g. 100% Black female owned firm. We offer services that will cater to an audience of creative, politically aware and multicultural African brands, institutions and businesses.
Through a world class digital platform we engage with companies and brands in the African region in sectors of news, music, politics, art and popular culture. The title 54 Republic is an name representing 54 states on the African continent. This signifies our desire to tell stories in all spheres of influence, championing values that make a positive difference and uplift the African narrative to the world, our clients, our partners and society at large.
